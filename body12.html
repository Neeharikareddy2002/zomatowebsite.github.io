Orissa Restaurants

Rasabali is a traditional sweet dish from the Indian state of Odisha. It is made from flattened balls of chhena (cottage cheese) that are deep-fried until golden brown, and then soaked in a sweet and creamy syrup made from milk, sugar, and cardamom. Mudhi mansa, kora khai, khaja, palua ladu, rasagolla and mahura are counted among the most famous foods of Odisha. Although most Odia dishes are prepared with minimum oil and spices, they are flavourful and can be easily relished by people of all ages.
