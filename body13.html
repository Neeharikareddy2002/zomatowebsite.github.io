Pune Restaurants

Puran Poli this is an iconic Maharashtrian sweet dish which is essentially a roti stuffed with puran (a sweet chickpea filling with nutmeg, cardemom and sugar). When in Pune, be sure to not miss out on delicacies like Misal Pav, Pithla Bhakri, Bhakarwadi, Mastani, Dabeli, Pav Bhaji, Poha, Vada Pav and baked goodies from the old school bakeries.
