Udaipur Restaurants

The word 'Diljani' refers to something close to your heart, and after tasting this Udaipur famous mithai, you'll undoubtedly have a space for it very close to your heart. Diljani sweet is a sweet with numerous little boondis mixed with Orange Juice; a single spoon of Diljani can satisfy all of your sweet tooths. Udaipur is one such city that is known for its delightful varieties of street delicacies, but with a twist of the mind-blowing spices of Rajasthan that together make up the best street food in Udaipur. From Daal Baati, Mirchi Badas, to common items like Kachoris and Dabelis, there is nothing you won't find in the glittering streets of this Golden City.
