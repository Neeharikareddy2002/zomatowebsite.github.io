Surath Restaurants

Surat is also famous for its drool-worthy street food. Alluring travelers from faraway locations, this destination never ceases to impress its visitors. There is a famous Gujarati saying “Kashi nu maran ne Surat nu Jaman” which means “dine in Surat and die in Kaashi” for a soul-stirring experience. There are numerous roadside stalls where you can relish the “Oh-So-Yummy” street food of Surat. If you are a food aficionado, then this is the perfect destination for you! You will find the visitors to be standing in the long cue to taste scrumptious meals.
